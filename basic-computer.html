Basic Computer Courses

Build rock‑solid digital literacy: Windows fundamentals, typing, file management, internet & email, and Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).

What You'll Learn

  • Computer basics: OS navigation, settings, security, and file organization.
  • Typing practice and productivity shortcuts.
  • MS Word for professional documents and formatting.
  • MS Excel for data entry, formulas, and charts.
  • PowerPoint for clear, confident presentations.
  • Internet safety, search techniques, email etiquette, and cloud drives.

Who It's For

Students, job seekers, office staff, and anyone new to computers.

Duration

4–8 weeks with flexible schedules (weekday/weekend batches).

Outcomes

Confident everyday computer use and office application proficiency.
